### Evaluation

Your submission will be evaluated against the [Arctic Ocean Oscillation Index (AOO index)](https://www2.whoi.edu/site/beaufortgyre/results/arctic-ocean-oscillation-index-aoo/updated-aoo-index/).

From the submitted isotype data, this is calculated as follows

$\text{AOO} = \frac{\hat{y}}{3} $, where $\hat{y}$ is the submitted data.
